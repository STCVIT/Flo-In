We bring to you the smartest way to login

Welcome to FLO-IN

FLO-IN is a browser extension that recognises a user when he or she attempts to log into one of their accounts. Only the user who has registered his or her face will be able to access the accounts. Every time the user tries to access one of his or her accounts, the user's face will be recognised. Option to open the accounts via pin is also provided.

As a result, there is no need to log out of your account every time to keep your data safe. With a single click you can access your accounts as well as keep the account password protected.

FLO-IN For Ease

PIN Log-In

Logging-In with Pin can also be an easy option sometimes, so FLO-IN provides you with an easy to access Pin Protection.

Facial Log-In

With the Face Detection Method, FLO-IN ensures the security of your accounts. By recognising your face it will log you into your account.

Auto-Fill

FLO-IN has a feature that allows you to autofill your credentials just by recognising your face. You won't have to retype your passwords; instead, you'll be able to log in with a single click.
