FLO-IN is a Chrome Extension that helps you easily Log-In and Log-Out of your accounts. It is also safe to use as it gives access to only the authoried user.

Add Extension To Chrome

About

Welcome to FLO-IN

FLO-IN is an extension that recognises a user whenever he/she tries to Log-In any of the accounts. The user who has registered his/her face will only be given the option to access the accounts. The face recognition will be done every time the user tries to access any of the accounts. Option to open the accounts via pattern is also provided.

Hence, there is no need to Log-Out every time of the account to keep the data safe. Without any single click you can access your accounts as well as keep the account password protected.

Features

FLO-IN For Ease

Pattern Log-In

Loggin-In with Pattern can also be an easy option sometimes, so FLO-IN provides you with an easy to access Pattern Protection.

Facial Log-In

FLO-IN provides you security of your accounts with Face Detection Method. You can Log-In to your accont without Logging-Out by recognising your face.

Auto-Fill

There is no need for a single click to fill up your passwords, FLO-IN provides you an option to Auto-Fill the passwords of your accounts.
